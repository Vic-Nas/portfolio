Welcome to My World

If you were to ask me to describe myself in one word, I would choose "Heritage" – a perfect blend of rich traditions and forward-thinking aspirations.

My name is Jean Hossou, the youngest child in my family, born and raised in Cotonou, Benin. To understand who I am, you need to understand where I come from. Let me take you on a journey to the place that shaped me.

Benin isn't just where I was born – it's woven into every aspect of my identity. From the vibrant streets of Akpakpa to the rhythmic music that fills our celebrations, my heritage is the foundation of who I am today.

My Roots

I was born in Cotonou, specifically in the vibrant neighborhood of Akpakpa – a place where community and tradition thrive together. As the youngest in my family, I was surrounded by stories, wisdom, and cultural richness from my earliest days.

Red Star, Akpakpa

The bustling center of my childhood neighborhood, where I spent countless hours exploring and learning.

Dantokpa Market

West Africa's largest market, where the colors, sounds, and aromas shaped my understanding of commerce and community.
